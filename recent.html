Bibliographic Details of Some Publications

This is mostly of historical interest, since it's generally easy to find bibliographic citations of papers nowadays. Some of the papers below are linked to the DBLP entry for a publication. A small number of papers don't have entries in DBLP and/or I thought might be hard to look up, so here's information on those citations.
