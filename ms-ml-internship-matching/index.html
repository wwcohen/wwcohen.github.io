Master of Science in Machine Learning: Internships

The program: The Master of Science in Machine Learning is part of CMU's Machine Learning Department, which is made up of a multi-disciplinary team of faculty and students across several academic departments. Machine learning is dedicated to furthering the scientific understanding of automated learning, and to producing the next generation of tools for data analysis and decision making based on that understanding.

The MS program in Machine Learning is a small and extremely selective three-semester program. Students in the program follow a rigorous curriculum, similar to curriculum used by the PhD students in machine learning, and interact closely with the Machine Learning faculty on research projects. MS students are also encouraged to use the summer after their second semester for an internship.

Students available for internships: No students are still available for internships (as of April 20th, 2015). If you would like to advertise an internship for next summer to our students, please send a one-paragraph description of your position by email to Diane Stidle (diane@cs.cmu.edu).
