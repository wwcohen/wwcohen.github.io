William's Academic Lineage

My advisor was Alex Borgida. His advisor was Ray Perrault. Perrault had two advisors, Joyce Friedman and William Rounds.

Bill Rounds' academic ancestry goes back to Poisson, Lagrange, Euler and other limunaries including Leibniz. Joyze Friedman's goes back through Quine, Alfre Whitehead, and eventually Isaac Newton and Galileo.
